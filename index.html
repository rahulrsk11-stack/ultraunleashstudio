About the Studio

Built on drawings that hold weight — literally and visually.

Ultra Unleash Studio is a boutique design practice working across architecture, interiors and visualization — where every concept is tested against structure before it's ever rendered.

We don't separate the idea from the structure that holds it up.

RS

Rahul Sultane

Founder & Principal

Civil engineer turned architectural designer, Rahul founded Ultra Unleash Studio to close the gap between structural drawings and the spaces people actually feel. Based in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, his work spans residential, commercial and interior projects across Maharashtra.

Ultra Unleash Studio began with a simple frustration: too many beautiful renders never survive contact with a structural drawing, and too many sound structures never get drawn beautifully. We exist in the middle of that — where civil engineering discipline meets the eye of an interior designer and the patience of a visualizer.

Every project on our boards passes through the same three lenses. First, does it stand — structurally, financially, practically? Second, does it feel right to move through — light, material, proportion? Third, can a client see it clearly before a single brick is laid?

That three-part process is why clients come to us for everything from a single working-drawing set to a full residence designed, rendered and detailed from the ground up.
